Career Resources

Career and Job Resources

IEEE has many career resources that help build your professional identity and plan your career.

To help students find entry level jobs, IEEE Student Job Site is a very useful tool.

To find how much you are worth, the IEEE USA Salary Service is a handy tool.

Continuing Education Resources

For continuing education, there are many certification and programs that IEEE offers. IEEE Professional Certifications page offers certifications in biometrics, software development, and wireless communication engineering.
