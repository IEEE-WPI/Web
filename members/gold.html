IEEE After Graduation

Graduates Of the Last Decade (GOLD)

Recent graduates have a vast amount of member benifits though IEEE membership. With the IEEE GOLD membership, the benifits include webinars, mentoring, networking, and volunteer leadership development. These allow new professionals to enhance technical knowledge and be apart of the IEEE community

Monthly Webinars are done with a wide range of topics from professional development to technical knowledge and humanitarian initiatives. Webinars are presented by experts and recorded with on-demand viewing.

Mentoring allows higher-grade IEEE members the oppertunity of enhancing their career skills through peer giadance. Young professionals can become a mentee and form professional development partnerships with IEEE members and learn career-enhancing skills.

With IEEE memberNet, IEEE members can be found by name and member grade. Serching for IEEE members based on technical and qeographical locations, and more.
